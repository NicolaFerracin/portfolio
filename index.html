# My work at Raisin

I joined Raisin in 2016. I started as a junior frontend developer and through the years I've had the chance to tackle interesting problems together with my team and to grow immensely as a developer, up to becoming a software engineer.

## Main Achievements

### Responsiveness ~ 2016/2017

Brought our customers facing web app to mobile users, applying responsive web design techniques across the whole app, starting from a completely non-responsive state.

### Investment Products React app ~ 2017/2018

Started the company's move toward a modern tech stack by developing a new client-side React application, communicating with the backend via a RESTful API.

### React migration ~ 2018/2019

Migrated the Java/JSP monolith legacy app over to React. The project accomplished the full decoupling of frontend and backend, which resulted in a smoother development process, higher maintainability, less bugs and improvement in hiring thanks to the use of modern technologies.

### Testing ~ 2018/2019/2020

Increased unit test coverage, from single digit to over 70%.

Migrated integration tests from Selenium to Cypress.

### Working Remotely ~ 2018/2019/2020

In January 2018 I started working remotely full-time while living in different cities around the world. Even with considerable time difference (at times up to 9 hours), I was always able to deliver features, meet deadlines and be an active part of my team. 2020 is my third year as a remote member of my team.
